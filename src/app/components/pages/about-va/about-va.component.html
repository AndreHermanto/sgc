About Variant Atlas

Variant Atlas is a controlled-access, web-based interface for querying genomic and phenotypic data for cohorts in the Australian Genomics Health Alliance (Australian Genomics).

Variant Atlas is hosted by the Garvan Institute of Medical Research.

The platform combines participants genomic, and links a participantâ€™s variant data to a selection of clinical information collected at recruitment. With this, approved users can explore genomic characteristics at the cohort-level, query specific genes or variants of interest across a cohort, and explore variants in subgroups with specific clinical phenotypes.

Summary - Explore - Clinical Filtering - Beacon

'Summary': Discover and compare interactive summary data on genes and variants in a Flagship

'Explore': Explore genes and variants across the Flagship

'Clinical Filtering': Filter patient data according to clinical traits*

*Restricted Access

'Beacon': Query the world's cohort datasets for specific variants
